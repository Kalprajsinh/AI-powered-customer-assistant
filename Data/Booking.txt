MG Motor India's booking flow allows customers to e-book vehicles via their official website or the My MG App. The process involves selecting a model/variant, entering personal details, choosing a dealership, paying the booking amount, and receiving a unique Booking ID for tracking. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]  
Steps to Book an MG Car Online: 

1. Visit Site: Go to the MG Motor India e-booking page. 
2. Select Vehicle: Choose the desired model, variant, exterior color, and interior options. 
3. Enter Details: Enter personal information (Name, Mobile, Email) and verify via OTP. 
4. Select Dealership: Enter the city pincode to select the preferred dealership. 
5. Payment: Confirm the booking amount and make the payment online. 
6. Confirmation: A unique Booking ID is generated, sent via email/SMS for tracking. [1, 3, 4]  

Key Aspects of the MG Booking Flow: 

• Tracking: Customers can track their booking status using the Online Booking Tracking tool. 
• App Usage: The My MG App allows for service booking, vehicle pickup, and tracking. 
• Pre-Reservation: Optional, allowing customers to receive a unique ID to prioritize their booking. 
• Finalization: Customers can use the Online Booking Completion page to finalize, add finance options, or buy accessories. 
• Cancellation: If needed, booking cancellation can be done online, with specific rules for older bookings. [2, 4, 6, 7, 8]  

Note: All bookings are subject to availability and variant choices. [9]
