The MG Motor India test drive process allows customers to experience vehicles either by visiting a dealership or booking a doorstep test drive online. The process is designed to be fully digital, enabling booking and coordination via the MG website. [1, 2, 3, 4]  
Steps to Book a Test Drive 

1. Visit the Website:  Navigate to the "Book a Test Drive" section on the MG Motor India website or MG Select website 
. 
2. Enter Details: Provide your first name, last name, mobile number, email ID, and postal code. 
3. Select Vehicle & Location: Choose your preferred MG model (e.g., Hector, ZS EV, Comet EV, Astor, Gloster) and the nearest dealership. 
4. Schedule Time: Select a convenient date and time for the test drive. 
5. OTP Verification: Verify your details using the OTP sent to your mobile/email. 
6. Confirm Request: A representative will get in touch with you to facilitate the test drive, either at the showroom or via a doorstep visit. [1, 5, 6, 7, 8]  

Key Things to Know 

• Safety Measures: MG provides "Disinfect and Deliver" services, where test drive vehicles are disinfected before arriving at customers' homes. 
• Documentation: You will typically need to present a valid driver’s license, and in some cases, your personal details might be used for KYC validation during the inquiry. 
• Pre-Drive Check: During the test drive, check for comfort features like legroom, seat height adjustment, and cabin comfort. Test the vehicle on both, or either, open and congested roads. 
• Consultation: Post-drive, you can consult with MG representatives regarding queries about the car's performance. [2, 7, 9, 10]  

For further assistance, you can contact the MG customer service centre or use the "My MG" app. [2, 3, 11, 12]
