MG Motor provides a comprehensive, technology-driven service process, largely managed through the My MG App, which allows users to book services and arrange vehicle pickup. Key aspects include 24/7 Roadside Assistance, maintenance plans (Shield Protect), and "Service on Wheels" for home-based maintenance, ensuring a seamless, digital, and customer-focused experience. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]  
Key Components of the MG Service Process 

• Booking and Scheduling: Services are booked via the  My MG App 
 or official website, with options to choose dates, times, and select pickup services. 
• MG Service on Wheels: A mobile workshop initiative that offers basic repairs and maintenance, such as oil changes and filter replacements, at the customer's home. 
• Roadside Assistance (RSA): 24x7 service for vehicle immobilization, accessible via a dedicated helpline (1800 100 6464). 
• Maintenance Plans (Shield Protect): Comprehensive protection plans for up to 5 years, starting at ₹26,000. 
• Vehicle Inspection: For certified pre-owned cars, MG conducts a rigorous 160-checkpoint inspection. 
• Digital Integration: The MG Xperience Cloud and SAP DBM system track maintenance needs and ensure efficient service management. 
• Service Support: Specialized training for electric vehicle maintenance (e.g., ZS EV) ensures proper care of batteries and components. [1, 2, 4, 6, 7, 8, 9]  

For more details, including specific maintenance schedules for your vehicle model, it is recommended to check the  owner's manual 
 provided by MG.
